Our Mission

To promote health and equality for young people living with HIV/AIDS.

Marketing

Promoting the SKFOUNDATION via social media platforms to show our great work and get potential volunteers and potential donations in order to save these lives.

People we help

Shifra Kirabo foundation is non-profit organization that targets patients living with HIV/AIDS from age 5 to age 25 years mainly in rural areas.

SEO

A lot of children and the youth at large are facing the same challenges, so let's stand together for zero stigma and discrimination associated with HIV/AIDS.

Our Vision

A world where young people living with HIV/AIDS are enabled to live a healthy stigma free and successful life.
