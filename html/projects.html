Introduction to new nutrition

Nutrition is an important part of supporting adherence to HIV treatment and tolerance of side effects. Some youth living with HIV/AIDS- especially those living below the poverty line-do not have access to proper nutrition. We plan to support proper nutrition for youth living HIV/AIDS through:
o Raising chicken where these people maybe eating eggs for.
o Protein and also sell for generating money to their cooperative banks to help them with other things.
o Buying a plot of land where they can grow nutrition food such as vegetables and they can sell the remaining one.
